FARM TO KITCHEN


THE FARMS

Our farmers are carefully selected and educated to use environmentally sustainable farming techniques. All you see is organically farmed to give you the highest quality product, from the chemical-free soil to the hand-picked grains. The soil quality, cropping pattern, crop rotation, and nutrient levels in the soil are all examined.We make certain that the chosen area has adequate weather and temperature conditions when doing so. We use a variety of productive and regenerative practises such as soil fertility management, seed selection, biodiversity, composting, water management, crop rotation, and protection.

FACTORIES

We have an effective but reliable way of working thanks to a blend of conventional and cutting-edge machinery. For pest control, all goods are kept in a sanitary and C02 fumigated condition. All of our goods maintain their full potency thanks to our innovative practises and dehydration technologies.To prevent phytochemical breakdown, our products are dehydrated in a dry, sterile setting. We have a specialised mechanism that grinds all of our products at low temperatures to retain the matrix of bioactive molecules they contain. The final goods we deliver to you are of the highest quality, pure, and normal.
